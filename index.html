Band Bio

This band is originally from Agoura Hills, CA. They have been active since 1996. I began listening to them when I was in 6th grade and really enjoyed the mix between rap and rock. While I have not kept up with them, I do know that the sad news that the lead singer Chester Bennington, commited suicide in 2017. Being famous and successful does not stop one from feeling sad. We must always be cognizant of this, even with the people we see as the most happy.
